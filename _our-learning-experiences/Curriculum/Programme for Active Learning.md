<h2><strong>Programme for Active Learning</strong></h2>
<p>Zhonghua Primary School has forged many partnerships with various local
organisations and groups to support our school’s vision and mission. We
endeavour to nurture and develop our students into responsible, reflective,
resilient and caring learners. With the school motto in mind, “Diligence
Before Success”, Zhonghua Primary School works towards achieving our goal
by engaging and collaborating with partners-in-education; School Advisory
Committee, Parent Support Group <a href="https://zhonghuapri.moe.edu.sg/useful-links-and-information/parent-volunteers-zest/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">ZEST</a>,
external agencies, institutes of higher learning, the community and <a href="https://go.gov.sg/zpsalumni" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Alumni</a> as
we believe that it takes a village to raise a child.</p>
<p>These partners support the school by designing programmes to develop our
students in their academic, well-being, interest, character development
and social-emotional learning needs.</p>
